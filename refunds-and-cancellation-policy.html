Refunds & Cancellation Policy

  • Refunds are available within 7 days of course enrollment, provided less than 2 live sessions have been attended.
  • To request a refund, email us at support@gaurcoder.com with your enrollment details.
  • Refunds will be processed within 10 business days after approval.
  • After 7 days or attending more than 2 live sessions, refunds are not available.
  • Course cancellation requests must be made via email.
  • We reserve the right to cancel a course and issue full refunds if minimum enrollment is not met.

For any questions, please contact us via the Contact page.
